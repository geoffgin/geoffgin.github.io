SIADS Milestone II: Exploring the Impact of Pre-Existing Health Conditions on Various Diseases

This project builds upon Geoffreyâ€™s Milestone I project, focusing on cardiovascular mortality. It explores the role of pre-existing conditions and their correlation with cardiovascular and COVID-19 mortality. Utilized the CDC's Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) as the primary dataset. Employed regression, random forest, and K-Nearest Neighbors models for analysis. Unsupervised analysis involved PCA and k-means clustering to identify natural groupings of conditions.
